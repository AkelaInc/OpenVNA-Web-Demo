High-Pass Filter

This is a commercially available Mini-Circuits high-pass filter, a SHP-1000.

Band-Pass Filter/Diplexer

This is two ports on a custom CDMA cellular phone diplexer. VNA Port 1 is connected to the high-frequency diplexer port, and VNA port 2 is connected to the composite diplexer port. The low-frequency diplexer port is simply terminated.

Coupled Antennas

This is two cavity-backed planar log-periodic antennas designed and manufactured by Akela that are closely coupled to each other. A rotating metallic vane is periodically inserted between the two coupled antennas.
